Each show features a different 'look'. The result is a series of stunning rooms, all bearing the mark of Colin and Justin's unique and inventive design. Learn how to create a lavish baroque dining room, an elegant vintage boudoir, a chilled out Moroccan lounge, or an oriental style bedroom. Delve deep into the stylish world of design and interiors in this fabulous, jam packed series.
